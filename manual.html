I. General Information

    A. Safety and warnings

    • Do not pour liquids on the table
    • Do not expose the table to temperatures above 85 ºC
    • Do not sit on the table or put weights over 20 Kg over the table
    • Do not attempt to open the table and tamper with its electronics

II. Getting started

    A. Setting up the Playing Table

    • Unpack all the table components and assemble its legs using the bolts included. Put it in a place that accommodates the user’s needs. The sensors, led matrix and bone conductor are already integrated in the table top.

    B. Turning on and off

    • Connect the table to the power to turn on the device.

    C. Getting started with the led matrix

    • The led matrix serves as the primary component for playing games such as tic tac toe. First, open the playing table app on your smartphone. The image below is the app's main page.

Here it's displayed the matrix layout, as well as the different available colors. To turn it on, press “Connect Bluetooth” and select the playing table’s interface.

After the led matrix is on, each cell of the matrix can be controlled through the touch sensitive top of the table or by pressing the desired cell on the mobile app.

  • To select the color of each cell,  choose a color from the list below de matrix layout. After doing so, press the corresponding button of the desired color.
  • To turn on or off all the cells of the matrix, toggle the button “All” on or off, respectively.
  • To clear the previous choice of color for the cells and choose a new color, press the “Clear” button and press the press the corresponding button of the new color.

i) Tic-tac-toe

The objective of tic-tac-toe is to connect three noughs or crosses (Os or Xs depending on the player), in a vertical, horizontal or diagonal row. The first player to accomplish this, wins the game.

    E. Bone Conductor

    • To listen to some music, you can take advantage of the bone conductor capabilities integrated on the table.

To do so:

    • Go to the playing table app and connect to the table by pressing “Connect Bluetooth” and select the playing table’s interface. This will turn on the led matrix.
    • Select the music interface
    • Select a song to play

    F. Playing table power management

  • The playing table doesn’t need to be charged. It’s a plug and play device. To turn it off, unplug the power cord.
