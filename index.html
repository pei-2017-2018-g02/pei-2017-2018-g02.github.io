PLAYING TABLE

THE INTERACTIVE

TABLE FOR EVERYONE.

INTERACTING WITH EVERYONE

INCLUDE IT IN YOUR LIFE

After a long day of hard work, relax in the comfort of your living room as you would, with the addition of this ordinary looking table that can alter the room ambient to your needs.

The playing table is a multimedia device with environment changing capabilities, that interacts with the user, as well as its surroundings.

THINKING OF YOU

Looks like an ordinary everyday table, but it does much more. You can play various games, dim the surrounding lights, or even play a musical instrument.

RISKS AND ISSUES

The Playing Table may be broken.

The sensors may not work as expected or accordingly, if the users are too hyperactive.

The readings retrieved must be within a certain margin of error, and multiple readings must be done for more accurate and precise data.
